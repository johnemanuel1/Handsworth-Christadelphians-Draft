Your first visit

What to expect

You are welcome to simply come along, listen and get to know us.

01

A friendly welcome

Someone will greet you and help you find your way. There is no need to book or let us know in advance.

02

Simple Bible teaching

We read from the Bible, pray and sing together. You are free to join in as much or as little as you wish.

03

Time to talk

Light refreshments are served between our Sunday services, with plenty of time for questions and conversation.

Find us in Handsworth

Our Sunday services are held in the Science Block at KE VI Handsworth School for Girls. There is time for light refreshments and conversation between the services every Sunday.
