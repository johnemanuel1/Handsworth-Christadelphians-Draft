Safeguarding

Everyone should be treated with care and respect

We take concerns about the safety and wellbeing of children, young people and adults seriously.

Raising a concern

Please tell us

If you have a safeguarding concern connected with Handsworth Christadelphians, please email info@handsworthchristadelphians.com. State that your message concerns safeguarding so it can be directed appropriately.

If someone is in immediate danger, call the emergency services on 999. Concerns can also be reported directly to Birmingham Children's Trust or Birmingham City Council Adult Social Care.

Safeguarding concerns should not be delayed while waiting for a response from this website email address.

Our approach

Responding responsibly

We aim to listen carefully, treat concerns seriously, respect privacy and share information only where necessary to protect someone or meet legal responsibilities.

Detailed safeguarding procedures and contact arrangements are maintained for community activities. Please contact us if you need further information before attending with a child or adult who may require additional support.
