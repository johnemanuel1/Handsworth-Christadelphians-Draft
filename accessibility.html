Accessibility

Helping everyone take part

We want our website and meetings to be welcoming and usable for as many people as possible.

Website accessibility

Designed for clear reading

The website uses clear headings, keyboard-accessible navigation, readable colour contrast, descriptive image text and responsive layouts for phones and larger screens. English and Farsi versions are available.

If you find content difficult to access, encounter a broken link, or need information in another format, please email us and describe what would help.

Visiting in person

Ask before your visit

Our Sunday meetings are held in the Science Block at KE VI Handsworth School for Girls. Because individual access needs and venue arrangements can vary, please contact us before visiting if you need step-free access, seating assistance, help finding the room or other support.

We will explain the current arrangements as clearly as possible and help you plan your visit.
