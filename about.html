Who we are

A family of Bible believers

Handsworth Christadelphians are a Christian community based in Birmingham. We meet each week to remember Jesus, read and discuss the Bible, pray, sing and support one another.

Our members come from different cultures, ages and walks of life. We value the warmth and variety this brings to our community, and we try to make sure that visitors quickly feel at home.

Our name

What does Christadelphian mean?

The name Christadelphian comes from two Greek words and means brothers and sisters in Christ. Christadelphians have been meeting in Birmingham and around the world since the nineteenth century.

We share a common faith, but each local community organises its own meetings and activities. You may sometimes hear a local Christadelphian community called an ecclesia, a Bible word meaning an assembly or gathering.
