Common questions

Before you arrive

Can anyone attend?

Yes. Our public meetings are open to everyone, including people who are new to church or the Bible.

Do I need to book?

No. You are welcome to simply arrive before the meeting begins.

Do I have to take part?

No. You are welcome to listen and join in only as much as you feel comfortable.

Are children welcome?

Yes. Sunday School runs at 12:30pm alongside the Bible Hour.

Finding the room

Directions to the Science Block

Travel to KE VI Handsworth School for Girls on Hall Road, Birmingham, B21 9AR. The Google Maps link above leads to the school site.

Enter the school grounds from Hall Road and look for, or ask for, the Science Block. Please allow a little extra time on your first visit to find the correct building.

If you would like help finding the room or need to discuss step-free access or another access requirement, please email us before your visit.
